- Share a note with someone. Open a note, tap the ⋯ menu, choose "Share with someone…" and pick a recipient in iMessage / Mail / wherever. Both of you can edit it from then on; changes sync across iCloud.
- Shared notes show a small person glyph and a thin warm border on the cards list, so you can tell at a glance which notes someone else can see.
- Receive a shared sticky from someone else: tap the iMessage link, accept, and it lands in your list alongside your own notes.
- Window state (rolled-up / closed) is now per-device. If you collapse a note on your Mac it no longer changes the height on your iPhone or for the person you're sharing with.
